Data

Our datasets are sourced from the Spotify API and the Twitter API. The Spotify data is composed of three primary datasets and three supplementary datasets. The first primary dataset is the top 100 new featured albums from six majority English-speaking marketplaces, five majority Spanish-speaking market places and one country with no large majority language (South Africa). From these albums, we created another dataset of all the artists and their corresponding information, including a list of their top ten tracks. Another one of our core Spotify datasets is information about each of these top ten tracks and the tracks on each album. Our Twitter dataset contains the thirty most recent tweets from each of the featured Spotify artists along with metrics like number of followers, favorites and retweets.
